Red and White with Disaster All Over it

Success you were able to apply to a secret agency! Now it is time to get dressed for your interview. But disaster struck in the community laundry room, The School of Mines decided to invest in a prototype laundry automation robot for the entire college campus. What was a good idea to help students has turned into the complete opposite! The laundry robot malfunctioned and tossed your interview clothes and everyone on campus clothes into huge piles.

Instead of sorting out the clothes by yourself and miss your interview. You make use of the laundry robot and the data it has collected. To find your interview clothes you are tasked with analyzing the data the robot is gathering. The robot gives you two string arrays. One array has a list of clothing types, that is shirt, pants, socks etc. The other array is the list of colors of these items.

Objective

Use the two lists to answer the following questions:

1) How many shirts of each color are there?

2) Assuming a pair of socks is two socks of the same color, how many pairs of socks do you have?
