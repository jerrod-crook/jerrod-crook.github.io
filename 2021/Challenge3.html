Wolfram Beta Lite

Natural language processing is the science of deriving meaning from text, and is becoming increasingly prevalant in software, especially with digital assistants like Siri and Alexa. This challenge is just the tip of the iceberg and focuses on math equations.

Your goal for this challenge is to write a program that can read the MathProblems.txt file, evaluate the 25 math problems listed in it, and output their answers.

The problems use addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, as well as parentheses, so you'll need to be careful to follow the proper order of operations.

Do not use a piece of code that evaluates a string as if it were code, such as Python's eval() function, as this is unsafe, and do not use any third party libraries to automate this.
