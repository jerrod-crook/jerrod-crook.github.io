Details

Tyler Tech has developed 6 coding challenges in order of varying difficulty. They are all language agnostic, meaning you can use any programming language that you like

When you think you have the answer raise your hand and we will confirm your answer. (We may ask to see your code/notes).

Wrong answers do not count against you, so guess as much as you like

With every correct answer your entire team may choose some SWAG

All questions are found on this website. Feel free to ask us clarifying questions and use the internet as you see fit

You will have 4 hours to code with an hour lunch inbetween


How To Win

Whoever has the correct answers for all 6 challenges first will be the winner

In the event that no team completes all the challenges, the winner will be whoever finished the most challenges the earliest

Second place follows the same rules


Optimization - $100 Prize

We have an additional way to win a cash prize

For challenge number 2 (calculator challenge). We are looking for the most optimized solution (fastest) for a very large mystery number

(To be clear instead of 0 to 1,000,000 we will replace 1,000,000 with a much larger number)

To win you must produce the correct solution and print out the start and end time, or the total time

You must also run your solution on the computer lab computers

We will reveal the mystery number near the end of the competition

Schedule
  • 10ish to 12 - CODE
  • 12 to 1 - EAT
  • 1 to 3ish - CODE

Rules
  • Only 4 participants per team
  • You can not code during lunch, but you can talk about the problems
  • Every team is eligible for both finishing 1st or 2nd and the Optimization Prize

Prizes
  • 1st Place - $400
  • 2nd Place - $250
  • Optimization Winner - $100
  • Correct Answers - SWAG of choice
