1-800-Flowers

Phone Numbers unlike memorizing your own phone number or your mother’s they are usually hard to remember. Hence why Billboards and businesses use letters within their number to make it more memorable.

Given the following text file of phone numbers (PhoneNumbers.txt) Translate the following phone numbers into their actual number digits.

Above is a picture of a keypad with the associated numbers that correlate to the letter above it. EX. “A” => 2, “Z” => 9, “R” => 7, “#” => 303 (or local area code)

Input Format:

1-800-FLOWERS

#-GEE-WIZ2

Output Format:

1-800-356-9377

303-433-9492
